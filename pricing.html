Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

You get a limited number of free sends to try all features before purchasing.

What counts as an "active sender"?

Each Google account that creates schedules or triggers counts as one sender. Recipients are unlimited.

How do I pay?

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can stop your subscription at any time. Open schedules and triggers will stop at the end of your billing period.
