Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Clear Approve ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our Google Sheets Add-on handles your data.

1. Limited Use Disclosure

Clear Approve's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. Data Access & Scopes

Our Add-on requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • Active Spreadsheet Access (currentonly): We read only the rows you explicitly select in the active spreadsheet to generate snapshots. We do not access your other files or Drive data.
  • Email Service: We use your Google account to send the snapshots you generate. We never send emails without your direct interaction (clicking the "Send" button).
  • External Requests: We connect to our secure licensing server solely to verify your subscription status and Pro features.

3. Data Usage & The "Snapshot" Feature

When you use the "Send Snapshot" feature, the following occurs:

  • The data from your selected rows is converted into HTML "Cards" or a PDF document.
  • Automatic Formatting: If you select more than 8 rows, the data is automatically converted to a PDF attachment to ensure email deliverability and document clarity.
  • This data is sent directly to your chosen recipient via the Google Mail API.

4. Data Storage & Security

We do not store your spreadsheet data. All row content is processed in real-time to generate your email and is not cached or saved on our servers. Your spreadsheet data stays within the Google ecosystem.

We only store your Email Address in our licensing database to manage your trial limits and Pro subscription.

Data Protection Mechanisms: We use industry-standard security protocols to protect your data during transit. All communications between the Clear Approve Add-on and our licensing servers are encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL/TLS) technology.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Clear Approve does not share, sell, or rent Google user data to third parties. Data is only transferred to the recipient's email address as explicitly directed by the user through the "Send Snapshot" action. We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. AI Disclosure

Clear Approve does not use your spreadsheet data to train artificial intelligence or machine learning models.

7. Contact Support

If you have questions about this policy, please visit our Support Page.
