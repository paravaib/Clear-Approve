Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Clear Approve operates entirely within your Google Workspace environment. We minimize data access to ensure your business remains secure.

Data Access & Usage

Clear Approve accesses only the active Google Sheet where it is explicitly enabled. The add-on reads row data to generate approval emails and writes status metadata back to your sheet.

Data Storage

All approval data and audit logs are stored only within your Google Spreadsheet. We do not store your spreadsheet content on our servers.

Google User Data Policy

Clear Approve's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
